Special Programs We offer

Juan R. Liwag Memorial High has multiple programs that you can join.

SPSTE

A special program for the development of Science, Mathematics and Research in Special Science Class (SSC).

SPA

A special program for the development for high school students with potential or talents in the Arts.

SPJ

A special program that develops learners with a talent in in writing and broadcasting.

SPS

A special program for the development of learners with potential and talent in Sports.

SPED

A special program that educates children in a way that accommodates their individual differences, disabilities and special needs.

SPFL

A special program that enhances learners' foreign language skills.
