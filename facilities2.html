Learning Centers

Juan R. Liwag Memorial High School introduces you to our School Learning Centers! JRLMHS has many learning centers such as ESP Learning Center, Araling Panlipunan(AP) Learning Center, TLE/ICT Learning Center, FILIPINO Learning Center, ENGLISH Learning Center, SCIENCE Learning Center, MAPEH Learning Center, and MATH Learning Center. It is a faculty room for teachers, teachers may also add additional interest areas such as computers and more. Teachers stay there to do paper works, reports, and the grades of students. Learning Centers can help teachers and students focus on a set of skills and deepen the knowledge and abilities in this area.
